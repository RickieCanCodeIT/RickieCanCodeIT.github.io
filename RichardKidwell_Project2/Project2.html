Geometry:

- Click on the left mouse button and move the cursor to rotate selected node.
- Q translates the node out, A translates the node in. This also scales the arm for circle nodes.
- E will scale the triangle nodes outwards, D scales them inwards.
- W zooms in, S zooms out.
- F moves the entity forward, B moves it backwards, L moves it to the left, R moves it to the right.
